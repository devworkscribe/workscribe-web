Privacy Policy

Transparency and privacy by design.

Last Updated: January 20, 2026

1. Introduction

WorkScribe ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our application functions regarding your personal data. The core philosophy of WorkScribe is "Local First". We designed the app so that your data remains on your device and is not collected, sold, or transmitted to us.

2. Data Collection & Usage

We do not collect any personal data.

All entries, images, project details, and statistics created within the app are stored locally on your device's internal storage in a secure database. We do not operate a cloud server, and we have no access to your information.

4. Third-Party Services & Data Collection

While WorkScribe itself does not collect your data, the app utilizes services provided by **Google** which may collect information independently when you interact with them.

Google Maps SDK

We use the Google Maps SDK to allow you to view your location-tagged entries on a map.

What is collected? When you view the map, your device connects directly to Google's servers. Google may collect:

  • Technical Information: IP address, device identifiers, and operating system version.
  • Location Data: Exact location data is processed by Google to render the map tiles relevant to your position.
  • Interaction Data: Search queries or map interactions (zooming/panning).

This collection is governed entirely by Google's Privacy Policy. We encourage you to review their terms here: Google Privacy Policy.

No Other Analytics: Apart from the map functionality described above, we do not include any other third-party analytics (like Firebase Analytics, Facebook SDK, or Mixpanel) or crash reporting tools. Your journal entries and notes are never transmitted to third parties.

5. Data Safety & Retention

  • Retention: Since data is stored locally, it is retained indefinitely on your device until you delete the app or clear its data.
  • Deletion: You can delete individual entries or all app data via your device settings. Uninstalling the app permanently removes all data associated with it.
  • Encryption: Data is stored in the app's private directory, which is sandbox-protected by the Android OS and inaccessible to other apps.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 13. Since our app does not collect data, we do not hold data on children.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
